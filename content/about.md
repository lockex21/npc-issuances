This is an unofficial, independently maintained reference library of issuances, advisory opinions, decisions, resolutions, and orders of the [National Privacy Commission](https://privacy.gov.ph/) (NPC) of the Philippines.

## Source and status

- All documents are Philippine government public records, collected from the NPC's official website and reproduced here for research and citation convenience.
- Texts are extracted from official PDFs and manually cleaned. Extraction and cleanup errors are possible — **always verify against the official source before relying on a text**.
- This site is not affiliated with or endorsed by the National Privacy Commission.

## Official sources

- [NPC Advisories and Circulars](https://privacy.gov.ph/pips-and-pics/advisories-circulars/)
- [NPC Advisory Opinions](https://privacy.gov.ph/advisory-opinions/)
- [NPC Decisions](https://privacy.gov.ph/decisions-2/)
- [Data Privacy Act of 2012](https://privacy.gov.ph/data-privacy-act/)
