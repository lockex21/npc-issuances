## Known Bugs to Avoid (found during cleanup runs)

These are issues the skill's instructions don't explicitly prevent. Every agent doing cleanup must follow these rules in addition to the skill.

**1. No `---` before `### Footnotes`**
Do NOT place a horizontal rule (`---`) immediately before the `### Footnotes` heading. The signature block already ends with a `---`; a second one creates a blank visual section in Quartz. The footnotes heading follows directly after the copy-furnished list, with no extra `---`.

Correct structure:
```
Copy furnished:

[Party names]

### Footnotes

[^1]: ...
```

**2. Footnotes heading must be `###`, not `##`**
Use `### Footnotes`, not `## Footnotes`. Using `##` puts it at the same level as `## Source` and `## Decision Text`, which is wrong.

**3. OCR artifacts persist in long documents**
For files over ~800 lines, double-check that footnotes were actually cleaned and not just collected — broken lines (`NPC Case No. 20 -\n026`), spaced hyphens (`Complaints -Assisted Form`), and un-italicized citations (`Id .`) must all be fixed. Re-fetch the PDF if anything looks uncertain.
