NAGA , P.C.;

Before this Commission is a Complaint filed by HEB (Complainant)

against DA (Respondent) for an alleged violation of the Data Privacy

Act of 2012 or Republic Act No. 10173 (DPA). 1

Facts

In h is Complaints -Assisted Form (CAF), Complainant alleged that on

14 September 2020, Respondent posted on the ‘BELOVED NUEVA

VIZCAYA !!!’ Facebook page a call for people to report Complainant’s

whereabouts to the police and for the public to conduct a citizen arrest

on him. 2 According to Complainant, the post made by the Respondent

divulged sensitive details about the Complainant’s case filed against

him by Respondent’s partner .3 The said post stated:

PTPA.

Whoever knows where is. HEB can be informed of the police or

citizen arrest because he has a WARRANT OF ARREST in the

case of STAFA CC -XXX s MTC Bayombong.. he is a former

teacher NVGCHS.. Thank you very much [ .]
