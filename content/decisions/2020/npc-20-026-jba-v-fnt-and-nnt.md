Before this Commission is a complaint filed by JBA against FNT and

NNT for an alleged violation of Republic Act No. 10173 or the Data

Privacy Act of 2012 (DPA).

Facts

claimed that FNT and NNT committed identity theft “by using [her]

name in different website[s] with their pictures and contact

the complaint, the websites using her name we re still active. 2 As

evidence, JBA attached to her CAF various screenshots of website

advertisements ( ads ) containing her name. 3

from 2014 to 2017. 4 She only submitted her official resignation, on 30

October 2018. 5 On the same date, FNT received and signed the
