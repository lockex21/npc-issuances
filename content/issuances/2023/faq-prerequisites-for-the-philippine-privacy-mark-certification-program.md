## Issuance Text

**Circular No. 2023-05**

        FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQs) ON NPC CIRCULAR NO. 2023-05

## Prerequisites for the Philippine Privacy Mark (PPM) Certification Program

**SECTION I.** ***What does this Circular contain?*** It provides the prerequisites for certifying personal information controllers (PICs) or personal information processors (PIPs) and accrediting certification bodies (CBs) under the PPM Certification Program.

**SECTION II.** ***Are PICs and PIPs required to obtain PPM certification?*** No. The Circular only applies to PICs or PIPs that voluntarily seek certification and to CBs that voluntarily seek accreditation under the PPM Certification Program.

**SECTION III.** ***Are accreditation and certification the same?*** No. Accreditation is a third-party attestation related to a conformity assessment body that formally demonstrates competence to carry out specific conformity assessment tasks; CBs undergo accreditation so they can certify PICs and PIPs. Certification, on the other hand, is a third-party attestation related to an object of conformity assessment (product, process, service, system, installation, project, data, design, material, claim, person, body, or organization) with the exception of accreditation; PICs and PIPs are the ones who obtain certification with specific standards.

**SECTION IV.** ***Are the requirements the same for PICs and PIPs?*** Yes. Section 4 states that both PICs and PIPs must be certified against ISO/IEC 27001 (information security management system) and ISO/IEC 27701 (privacy information management system). The NPC will issue the detailed certification scheme, including specific requirements, rules, or procedures for certification.

**SECTION V.** ***What are the requirements for CBs?*** Section 5 states that CBs must be certified against ISO/IEC 27001 and ISO/IEC 27701 and must obtain accreditation based on ISO/IEC 17021-1.

**SECTION VI.** ***In what order must organizations obtain ISO/IEC 27001 and ISO/IEC 27701 certifications?*** ISO/IEC 27701 is an extension of ISO/IEC 27001, so organizations must secure the ISO/IEC 27001 certification before proceeding with ISO/IEC 27701.

**SECTION VII.** ***Why is ISO/IEC 17021-1 part of the requirements?*** ISO/IEC 17021-1 contains requirements for bodies engaged in auditing and certifying management systems, defining criteria for competence, consistency, and impartiality. The standard guarantees that CBs operate in a competent, impartial, consistent, and reliable manner.

**SECTION VIII.** ***Are there associated costs or fees for ISO/IEC 27001 and ISO/IEC 27701 certifications?*** Yes, costs arise when the interested participant undergoes certification with the chosen CBs on the specified ISO standards.

**SECTION IX.** ***What happens if PICs, PIPs, and CBs fail to complete the requirements under Sections 4 and 5?*** They shall not qualify to apply for certification or accreditation, as appropriate, under the PPM Certification Program.

**SECTION X.** ***Will the application be considered if it is a transferred ISO certification?*** Yes, the Circular does not prohibit transferring ISO certification from one CB to another.

**SECTION XI.** ***When was the Circular published in a newspaper of general circulation?*** It was published in the Daily Tribune on 15 March 2024.

**SECTION XII.** ***When is the effectivity of this Circular?*** It took effect on 15 March 2024, and as provided in Section 9, it becomes effective immediately after publication.
