                                   Section 20 (f) of the DPA requires PICs to notify their  
                                   affected data subjects of breaches in cases that fall under  
                                   mandatory breach notification. NPC Circular 16-03  
                                   (Personal Data Breach Management) provides the  
                                   procedure for breach notification.

                                   Section 4 of the Advisory further clarifies breach notification  
                                   where the affected data subjects are children. It provides,  
                                   “[i]n cases where the affected data subjects are children,  
                                   PICs shall notify both the children and their parents or  
                                   guardians.”
