## Resolution Text

IN RE : FYNAMICS LENDING

INC . OPERATING

PONDOPESO ONLINE

LENDING APPLICATION

-----x---------------------------------------x

NPC 19910

For violation of the

Data Privacy Act of 2012

On 05 November 2020, the Commission issued an Order granting the

request by Respondent Fynamics Lending, Inc. (Respondent) for a

thirty (30) day extension or until 15 November 2020 to submit the

documents requested following the Clarificatory Hearing held on 01

October 2020.

On 15 November 2020, Respondent filed a Motion for Additional Time

to File Compliance, requesting for another extension of ten (10) days

within which to comply with the 01 October 2020 Order of the

Commission. They state in their Motion:

3. The Respondent respectfully manifest (sic) that while it

endeavored to secure the required documents, it was

experiencing difficulty due to the resignation and layoffs of its

former employees. Moreover, its efforts were hampered by the

series of typhoon s which affected Metro Manila.

Considering the circumstances raised by Respondent and in the

interest of an exhaustive investigation, the Commission grants the

requested additional time to submit the required documents.

WHEREFORE, the Commission GRANTS a final and nonextendible

extension of ten (10) days or until 25 November 2020 for Fynamics

Lending, Inc. to produce and submit the documents specified in the

Order dated 01 October 2020.

Failure to comply with this Order shall cause the Commission to

adjudicate the issues on the basis of the evidence on record.

SO ORDERED.

City of Pasay, Philippines

19 November 2020. NPC 19910

In re: Fynamics Lending Inc.
